Our Story

Jemena Trading Limited was founded in 2020, a period marked by global upheaval and industrial uncertainty. As essential industries struggled with supply chain disruptions, we emerged as a trusted force in the chemical sector, ensuring a consistent supply of high-quality chemical solutions** to support industrial continuity.

We have established ourselves as a strategic partner for industries, providing cutting-edge chemical formulations, customized supply solutions, and expert consultancy. Our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and efficiency ensures that businesses receive tailored chemical solutions that align with their production needs and environmental goals.

Over the years, Jemena Trading Limited has built strong relationships across multiple sectors, including manufacturing, mining, water treatment, food processing, pharmaceuticals, and oil & gas. Our global partnerships with reliable suppliers allow us to offer safe, innovative, and compliant chemical solutions to drive industrial growth.

At Jemena Trading Limited, we not only just supply chemicals, we provide solutions! From on-time chemical shipments to repackaging services and expert chemical consultations, our mission is to ensure that businesses thrive with efficient and innovative solutions. Whether supporting large-scale manufacturers or niche industry players, we deliver exceptional service, technical expertise, and a commitment to sustainability.

As we move forward, our focus remains on expanding our capabilities, deepening industry partnerships, and continuously improving our service offerings. We are driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence, ensuring that every product we deliver meets the highest industry standards. Jemena Trading Limited is more than a supplier; we are an essential partner in industrial success.
