About Jemena Trading Limited

Jemena Trading Limited is your trusted partner in the supply of high-quality industrial chemicals, reagents, and specialty chemicals. With decades of experience, we have built a reputation for delivering tailored chemical solutions that empower industries to thrive.

Our comprehensive portfolio serves diverse industries such as water treatment, food production, mining, cosmetics, and more. Located in Industrial Area, Nairobi, Kenya, we’re dedicated to excellence, sustainability, and global compliance.

We pride ourselves on ensuring seamless delivery, flexible supply options, and personalized customer service, helping businesses achieve their operational goals with efficiency and reliability.
